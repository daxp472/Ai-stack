# Best Prompts for Research AI

10 ready-to-use prompts for source-backed, high-quality research.

## 1. Research Brief Starter [Beginner]
**Use case:** Kick off any topic with structured scope.

```text
You are a research analyst.
Topic: [TOPIC]
Objective: [OBJECTIVE]
Audience: [AUDIENCE]
Depth: [QUICK/MEDIUM/DEEP]

Build a research brief with:
- Key questions
- Subtopics to investigate
- Recommended source types
- Potential bias risks
- Suggested output format
```

## 2. Literature Review Accelerator [Advanced]
**Use case:** Build a concise review from multiple papers.

```text
Act as an academic research assistant.
Research question: [QUESTION]
Paper abstracts:
[PASTE ABSTRACTS]

Synthesize:
1) Core themes
2) Agreement vs disagreement points
3) Methodological strengths/limits
4) Gaps in current literature
5) Suggested next research directions

Do not invent citations.
```

## 3. Source Credibility Checker [Intermediate]
**Use case:** Evaluate trustworthiness of references.

```text
You are a credibility auditor.
Sources:
[PASTE LINKS OR SUMMARIES]

Rate each source on:
- Authority
- Evidence quality
- Recency
- Bias risk
- Verifiability

Return a scored table and recommendation: use / caution / reject.
```

## 4. Fact-Check Workflow Prompt [Intermediate]
**Use case:** Validate claims before publication.

```text
You are a fact-check editor.
Claims to verify:
[PASTE CLAIMS]

For each claim provide:
- Verification status
- Best supporting source
- Counter-evidence source
- Confidence level
- Suggested wording if uncertain

Prioritize primary or reputable secondary sources.
```

## 5. Competitive Landscape Mapper [Advanced]
**Use case:** Analyze market players quickly.

```text
Act as a market intelligence analyst.
Industry: [INDUSTRY]
Focus: [SEGMENT]
Region: [REGION]

Create:
- Top competitors list
- Positioning map
- Pricing model comparison
- Product differentiation summary
- Opportunity gaps for new entrants
```

## 6. Policy or Law Summary Simplifier [Beginner]
**Use case:** Understand dense legal/policy docs.

```text
You are a policy simplification assistant.
Document:
[PASTE TEXT]

Summarize into:
- Key points in plain language
- Who is impacted
- What changed vs before
- Practical implications
- Open questions to clarify with experts
```

## 7. Interview Research Pack [Intermediate]
**Use case:** Prepare deeply for expert interviews.

```text
Act as a journalist prep assistant.
Interview subject: [NAME/ROLE]
Topic: [TOPIC]
Audience outcome: [WHAT THEY SHOULD LEARN]

Generate:
- Background summary
- 12 high-quality interview questions
- 5 follow-up probes
- Potential controversial areas
- Fact-check checklist for post-interview
```

## 8. Thesis Statement Refiner [Beginner]
**Use case:** Sharpen academic or argumentative writing focus.

```text
You are an academic writing coach.
Topic: [TOPIC]
Current thesis draft: [DRAFT]
Evidence available: [LIST]

Rewrite thesis into 5 stronger options.
For each, include:
- Why it is stronger
- What evidence is needed
- Potential counterargument
```

## 9. Data-to-Insight Explainer [Advanced]
**Use case:** Convert raw findings into strategic insight.

```text
You are a research storyteller.
Findings:
[PASTE FINDINGS]
Decision context: [BUSINESS/ACADEMIC/POLICY]

Deliver:
- 5 key insights
- Why each matters
- Confidence level per insight
- Risks of misinterpretation
- Recommended next action
```

## 10. Research QA Before Publish [Intermediate]
**Use case:** Final review of a report or paper draft.

```text
You are a senior research reviewer.
Draft:
[PASTE DRAFT]

Audit for:
- Logical consistency
- Evidence alignment
- Overclaiming
- Missing citations
- Clarity for target audience

Return prioritized revisions and a cleaner final version.
```
